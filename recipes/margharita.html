Margharita

A staple of the industry, a classic margharita, you just can't hate it.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Tequila
  • Orange liqueur
  • Lime Juice
  • Lime wheel

Steps

  1. To a shaker, add tequila, orange liqueur and lime juice
  2. Fill with ice and shake for 30 seconds
  3. To serve, first sprinkle salt on a small plate
  4. Wet the rim of the cocktail glass and coat the rim with the salt
  5. strain the liquid into the glass after adding ice
  6. Top with lime wheel
