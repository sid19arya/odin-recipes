The classic mojito

If you are looking for a stable, look no further than the most classic cocktail outhere, a simple mojito. Rum, lemon and minty goodness all in one little glass.

Ingredients

  • Mint leaves
  • Lime juice
  • Simple Syrup
  • White rum
  • Soda water

Steps

  1. Muddle the leaves in a cocktail shaker
  2. To the same shaker, add lime juice, simple syrup and rum to your hearts content
  3. Fill with ice and shake for 30 seconds
  4. Place ice into a glace, strain liquid into glass
  5. top with soda water, and mint leaves if you're feeling really fancy
