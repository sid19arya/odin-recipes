whiskey_sour

Top of the Line classic. Cant go wrong with bourbon and orange.

Ingredients

  • Bourbon
  • Lemon Juice
  • Simple Syrup
  • Orange wheel
  • Marashino Cherry

Steps

  1. To a shaker, add bourbon, lemon juice and simple syrup
  2. Fill with ice and shake for 30 seconds
  3. Place ice into a glass, strain liquid into glass
  4. Top with orange wheel and cherry
